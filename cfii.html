What This Rating Lets You Do

Three new
privileges.

The CFI-I is an additional rating to your CFI certificate. It can also be completed as an initial certificate by adding training days. Either way, all maneuvers are flown from the right seat to PTS standards.

PRIV · 01

Instrument Rating Training

Provide the required ground and flight training for Instrument Rating candidates — including IFR cross-country, approaches, holding, and partial-panel work.

PRIV · 02

Endorse for the Practical

Endorse Instrument Rating candidates for the FAA oral and practical exams once they meet the ACS standards.

PRIV · 03

Conduct IPCs

Conduct Instrument Proficiency Checks for instrument-rated pilots — the recurrency credential for everyone who flies in the system.

What Is a Discovery Flight?

Honestly? A test drive.

A Discovery Flight is the cleanest way to find out if flight training is right for you — without committing to a $20K program first. Sim time, real flight time, and a real conversation. $325, all-in.

You start in the Redbird AATD to get oriented, then move to the actual DA40NG you'd train in. You're in the left seat — the pilot's seat. Your CFI handles takeoff and landing; everything in between is you on the controls.

What You'll Do

Three stages. One unforgettable day.

Show up at our FBO (Signature or ) 15 minutes early. The whole thing wraps in about two hours door-to-door — sim, flight, and debrief.

01
Redbird Sim
30 minutes
Climb into one of our two state-of-the-art Redbird AATD simulators. Your CFI walks you through the controls, the panel, and basic maneuvers — straight-and-level flight, gentle turns, climbs and descents — so you're already comfortable before you ever leave the ground.
02
Fly Charleston
1 hour airborne
A full hour at the controls of the Diamond DA40NG. CFI handles takeoff and landing — everything in between is you. Coastline, harbor, the bridges from above. Skills you just practiced in the sim, now over the real city. CFI is on the controls anytime you want.
03
Debrief & Next Steps
15 minutes
Logbook entry signed by your CFI (these hours count toward your PPL). Honest conversation about how the flight went, what training looks like from here, and whether CRAFT is the right fit. No hard sell. Photo from the cockpit, sent to you afterward.

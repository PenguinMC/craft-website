Meet the team.

Your CFI is the single biggest factor in how fast you finish. Here’s the bench you’ll be flying with at CRAFT — led by a 2,000-hour Director of Flight Operations.

Director of Flight Ops
★ Owner · CFI · CFII · MEI

Barry Emerson

Owner · Director of Flight Operations · Senior Flight Instructor

Barry has had a fascination with aviation from a young age. He obtained his CFII while attending The College of The Ozarks in the 1980’s. Barry worked as an executive in the automotive industry for most of 30 years, keeping in touch with his love of flying through instructing and acting as a contract pilot. He is now CRAFT’s Director of Flight Operations and Senior Flight Instructor with over 2,000 hours in the logbook.

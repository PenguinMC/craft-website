The CRAFT difference

CRAFT Flight Training & Simulation operates out of Signature Flight Support and Atlantic Aviation at Charleston International Airport (KCHS) — a Class C controlled-airfield environment with daily Part 121 traffic. We're not a sleepy grass strip. We train pilots where pilots actually work.

We chose Diamond aircraft (DA40NG and DA42-VI) for a reason: glass cockpits, Jet-A turbodiesels, and the same airframes regional carriers transition from. We invested in two Redbird AATD simulators because $70/hr beats $255/hr nine days out of ten. And we built our DPE relationships because waiting six months after passing your training is a disgrace.
